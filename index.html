Larry Yu

Pre-business

Born on December thirteen, two thousand and one, being the middle child of an older sister and a younger brother-my name is Larry Yu. I was always told that I was the laziest one out of my two siblings and even my cousins; which I have i admit, is true, unfortunately. I was also told that I was the smartest out of my siblings and cousins, I took somewhat of pride into that. However, despite knowing that I always let the laziness overcome my potential greatness at academics. It was always in the back of my head that I should be doing better than how I am doing, but I would just cast it aside a temporarily neglect that thought. I went to Logan Elementary School, which is now called “Logan Span School”, due to the integration of seventh and eighth grade. Elementary school was a time where I joked around with my friends, always playing and having fun; my concerns for my own grades always came second to be fair I was still very young, so you cannot really blame me right?My grades were never terrible, decent actually-having mostly Bs and a couple of As. My parents are Chinese, so they would expect a lot more out of me and so did I. At the end of every school year I would bring home a report card that was mostly Bs and my parents would say, “good job, but you know you can do better and get straight As next time”. The same sentence every single year… looking back, I am surprised at how my parents were not harder on me than they should have been. I know friends and family whom get scolded and cry and beat because they have a B on their report card. In a sense I took that for granted, not having the pressure and fear my peers had to deal with for years and years.
